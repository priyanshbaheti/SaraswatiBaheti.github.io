ABOUT US

She took care of all the children along with her mother. Her father had a very small grains shop in the city which could only make for their normal livelyhood. Still with the great efforts of his sibblings and mothers all her brothers and sisters are now in a very good economic and social position in the society. She herself is a P.hd in music and M.a. and B.ed. her two brothers are Chartered Accountants. She did a lot of struggle in her life.
